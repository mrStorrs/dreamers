# Discovery Questions — New Project

Use these to grill the user before writing anything. Have a real conversation — do not dump all questions at once. Push back on vague answers. "It depends" and "we'll figure it out" are not acceptable; press for specifics. If an answer opens a new question, ask it.

---

## Vision & problem

- What are you building? 
- What specific problem does it solve? Who has this problem today and how do they currently deal with it?

## Platform & constraints

- What platforms must it support at launch? (web, iOS, Android, desktop, API-only, all)
- Any hard tech constraints? (existing systems to integrate with, languages already known, things that are off the table)
- Is AI a core part of the product, a background dev tool, or not involved in the product at all?
- Any compliance or data residency requirements? (GDPR, HIPAA, SOC2, etc.)

---

## Grilling rules
- If the user's answers are internally inconsistent (e.g. "millions of users" + "SQLite"), surface the conflict immediately and resolve it before continuing.
